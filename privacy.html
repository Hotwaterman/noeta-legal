Noeta — Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Effective / 生效日期: 2026-07-01 · Contact / 联系: jyh824658571@gmail.com

English

Noeta ("we", "the app") is a private notebook that helps you keep notes about the people in your life and offers AI-assisted insights. We keep data collection to what is needed to run the app and give you control over your information.

1. Information we collect

Account information. Noeta works without an account. If you choose to sign in (Sign in with Apple, or mobile phone number), we receive a user identifier and, depending on the method, an email address (which Apple may relay on your behalf) or your phone number, used to secure your account and enable backup and sync.

Content you create. Your notes and related content are stored on your device. They are uploaded to our servers only if you turn on cloud backup / sync.

Usage & diagnostic data. Basic technical information about the app's requests (feature used, success/failure, timing, error type) so we can operate and improve the service. This does not include the content of your notes.

2. How we use information

To provide and maintain the app, including account sign-in and optional cloud backup/sync; to provide AI-assisted features (for which relevant content is processed by us and/or trusted third-party providers solely to produce the result); to keep the service reliable and secure; and to respond to your support requests. We do not use your content for advertising, and we do not sell your data.

3. Storage: local by default, cloud optional

By default, your content stays on your device. Cloud backup/sync is optional and becomes active only after you sign in and turn it on. When enabled, your content is stored on our servers so you can back it up and use it across your devices.

4. Sharing

We share information only with service providers that help us operate the app (such as cloud hosting and AI processing), under agreements limiting their use to providing services to us, or where required by law. We never sell your data.

5. Retention & deletion

Content stored on your device is removed when you delete the app or clear it in-app. If you enabled cloud sync, you can delete your cloud data in the app or by requesting account deletion at the contact below; we then delete the associated content, retaining only limited records required for security or legal compliance.

6. Security

We use encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS) and protect server-side data with access controls.

7. Your choices

You can use the app without an account, enable or disable cloud sync at any time, sign out, or request account and data deletion.

8. Children

Noeta is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region).

9. Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the effective date above.

10. Contact

中文

Noeta（下称"我们""本应用"）是一款帮助你私密记录身边的人、并提供 AI 辅助洞察的笔记应用。我们只收集运行应用所必需的信息，并让你掌控自己的数据。

1. 我们收集的信息

账号信息。本应用无需账号即可使用。如果你选择登录（通过 Apple 登录，或手机号登录），我们会获得一个用户标识；视登录方式，可能包含邮箱（Apple 可能以中转方式提供）或手机号，用于保护你的账号并开启备份与同步。

你创建的内容。你的记录及相关内容保存在你的设备上。只有当你开启云端备份/同步时，才会上传到我们的服务器。

使用与诊断数据。关于应用请求的基础技术信息（使用了哪个功能、成功/失败、耗时、错误类型），用于运维和改进服务。这不包含你记录的内容。

2. 我们如何使用信息

用于提供和维护应用，包括账号登录与可选的云端备份/同步；提供 AI 辅助功能（为此，相关内容会由我们和/或受信任的第三方服务商处理，仅用于产出结果）；保持服务稳定与安全；以及响应你的支持请求。我们不会将你的内容用于广告，也不会出售你的数据。

3. 存储：默认本地，云端可选

默认情况下，你的内容保存在你的设备上。云端备份/同步是可选的，只有在你登录并开启后才生效；开启后，你的内容会存储在我们的服务器上，以便备份并在多台设备间使用。

4. 信息共享

我们仅与帮助我们运营应用的服务商共享信息（如云托管、AI 处理），并通过协议限制其仅为我们提供服务之用；或在法律要求时共享。我们绝不出售你的数据。

5. 留存与删除

保存在设备上的内容，会在你删除应用或在应用内清除时移除。如果你开启了云端同步，可在应用内删除云端数据，或通过下方联系方式申请注销账号；我们随后会删除相关内容，仅保留出于安全或法律合规所需的有限记录。

6. 安全

我们采用加密传输（HTTPS/TLS），并以访问控制保护服务器端数据。

7. 你的选择

你可以不登录使用本应用、随时开启或关闭云端同步、退出登录，或申请注销账号与删除数据。

8. 儿童

本应用不面向 13 岁以下（或你所在地区最低年龄）的儿童。

9. 变更

我们可能更新本政策，并会修改上方的生效日期。
