Noeta (知人) — Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Effective date / 生效日期: 2026-07-01 · Contact / 联系: jyh824658571@gmail.com

English

Noeta ("the app", "we") helps you keep private notes about the people in your life and uses AI to turn those notes into profiles, event analysis, and chat suggestions. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

No account

Noeta has no sign-up or login. When you first open the app it creates a random, device-only identifier so your data stays scoped to your device. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, address book, or precise location.

Your data stays on your device

Your notes, the people you track, AI-generated profiles and analyses, and any imported images or files are stored on your device. We do not keep a copy on our servers and do not back them up to our servers.

AI features

Some features — profiles, event analysis, and chat — use AI. To produce a result, the relevant content you are acting on is sent to a third-party AI service, processed to generate the result, and returned to your device. We do not store that content, and it is never used for advertising or sold. If you would rather not have content processed this way, simply don't use those AI features; basic recording still works.

What we keep on our servers

Only technical metadata about the app's requests — which feature was used, success/failure, timing, and error categories — so we can run and fix the service. This does not include your notes or the AI results.

Voice and media

Voice transcription runs on your device and the audio file stays on your device. An imported image or file is sent for AI processing only when you use a feature that needs it (for example, importing a chat screenshot to organize by person).

What we do NOT do

No advertising, no selling or renting of your data, no cross-app or cross-website tracking, and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Retention and deletion

Your content lives on your device. Deleting the app, or using the in-app "clear all" action, removes it. Server-side technical metadata is kept for a limited period for operations and then discarded.

Security

Requests are made over encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS).

Children

Noeta is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region).

Changes

We may update this policy; we will revise the effective date above and, for material changes, surface a notice in the app.

Contact

Questions or requests: jyh824658571@gmail.com

中文

Noeta（知人，下称"本应用""我们"）帮助你私密地记录你在意的人，并用 AI 把这些记录整理成侧写、事件分析和沟通建议。本政策说明本应用如何处理你的信息。

无账号

本应用没有注册或登录。首次打开时会创建一个随机的、仅本机使用的标识，让数据限定在你的设备上。我们不收集你的姓名、邮箱、电话、通讯录或精确位置。

你的数据留在你的设备上

你的记录、你跟踪的人、AI 生成的侧写与分析、以及导入的图片或文件，都保存在你的设备上。我们不会在服务器上保留副本，也不会备份到我们的服务器。

AI 功能

部分功能——侧写、事件分析、对话——会用到 AI。为产出结果，你当前操作的相关内容会被发送给第三方 AI 服务处理、生成结果后返回你的设备。我们不会存储这些内容，也绝不用于广告或出售。如果你不希望内容被这样处理，不使用这些 AI 功能即可；基础记录仍可正常使用。

我们在服务器上保留什么

仅保留应用请求的技术性元数据——使用了哪个功能、成功/失败、耗时、错误类别——用于运维和排障。这不包含你的记录内容或 AI 结果。

语音与媒体

语音转写在设备本地完成，音频文件留在你的设备上。只有当你使用需要它的功能时（例如导入聊天截图以按人归类），导入的图片或文件才会被发送做 AI 处理。

我们不会做的事

不做广告，不出售或出租你的数据，不做跨应用/跨网站追踪，不接入第三方分析或追踪 SDK。

留存与删除

你的内容存在你的设备上。删除本应用、或使用应用内"清空全部"，即可移除。服务器端的技术元数据仅保留有限时间用于运维，之后清除。

安全

请求通过加密传输（HTTPS/TLS）进行。

儿童

本应用不面向 13 岁以下（或你所在地区最低年龄）的儿童。

变更

我们可能更新本政策；届时会修改上方生效日期，重大变更会在应用内提示。
